Eccentricity

‹

Eccentricity is the Keplerian element often represented by the symbol " e ". It is the unitless measurement of how elongated the orbit is compared to a completely circular orbit, with 0 being a circular orbit, 1 being a parabolic orbit, and greater than 1 is a hyperbolic orbit.
